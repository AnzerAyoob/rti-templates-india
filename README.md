RTI Templates – India 🇮🇳

Collection of ready-to-use formats under the Right to Information Act, 2005.

These templates help citizens, journalists, activists, and researchers request information from government departments in a structured and effective way.

All templates are simple, editable, and practical.

---

What is RTI?

The Right to Information (RTI) Act, 2005 allows any citizen of India to request information from public authorities.

Government departments are legally required to respond within the prescribed time limit.

RTI helps promote:

- transparency
- accountability
- good governance
- citizen awareness
